Eastwood Writers

 
We meet on Monday afternoons (term time only) from 2.15 until 4.15pm in Woodfarm High School, Thornliebank, Glasgow, and are always seeking new members. As amateur but enthusiastic writers, we encourage members to develop their writing skills. We expect members to write 1000 words each second week.

We are affiliated to the Scottish Association of Writers. This permits members to enter competitions and attend its annual conference. The competitions attract entries from all over Scotland. We can claim a fair share of the awards in recent years.
