New format of meetings

First hour
The first hour will be devoted to the reading of new stories (max. 1000 words) and comment/critique. Any stories for which there isn’t time can be read the following week, when any surplus time will be used to read stories on the next topic on the list. As a result, the total amount of new writing you will be asked to do will be much smaller than before.

Topics
The topic list for Sept-Dec 2018 is:
A development that changed the world
A Western
An unusual occupation
Was it a ghost?
The Armistice*
A story for children
*Armistice stories will be read on 12 November (the day after Armistice Day).

Please write about “A development that changed the world” for the first meeting
on 3 September, which is NOT a “bring as you please” meeting.

We encourage diversity in writing styles, so feel free – where the topic permits – to write in the genre of your choice e.g. short story, article, poem, dramatic sketch.

Second hour
You will be invited to write for 20 minutes on a topic chosen by one of the members the previous week. Stories will then be read and comment offered. No writing should be done in advance, but you may wish to give the topic some thought.
The topic for Monday 3 September is “It was a summer’s day”.
From time to time, part or all of the second hour will be used for sessions on writing skills (e.g. rules of short story writing, building character, creating atmosphere) and visits from guest speakers.
