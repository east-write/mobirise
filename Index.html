  Eastwood Writers

We meet on Monday afternoons (term time only) from 2.15 until 4.15pm in Woodfarm High School, Thornliebank, Glasgow. We are always seeking new members. As amateur but enthusiastic writers, we encourage our members to develop their writing skills with our challenging syllabus featuring a wide range of topics. We expect members to write 1000 words on a specific topic each week.

We are affiliated to the Scottish Association of Writers. This permits members to enter competitions and attend its annual conference. The competitions attract entries from all over Scotland. We can modestly claim a fair share of the awards.

if you require more information please send an email to our hon. secretary at gmckendry@aol.com
